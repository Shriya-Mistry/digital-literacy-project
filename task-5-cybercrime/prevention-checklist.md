# STAY SAFE ONLINE CHECKLIST

- Immediately report cyber fraud on the National Cyber Crime Portal(official online platform launched by the Government of India that allows citizens to report cybercrimes in a simple and centralized way).
- Call helpline number 1930 to report financial fraud quickly and try to block transactions.
- Never share OTPs, PINs, or passwords with anyone; not even someone pretending to be from your bank.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
- Do not scan random QR codes to receive money.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Limit sharing personal information online (address, phone number, etc.).
- Backup your important data regularly to protect against ransomware.
